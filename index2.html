HOW TO START YOUR OWN YOUTUBE CHANNEL

PS4

If you are using your PS4 and want to stream or record it, don’t worry, it is actually really easy. All you will need is a good pair of headphones and an editing software. This will help you to properly check your audio and be able to modify and cut your videos into sections that you will be needing. You may also be able to add different effects of your choice and check how smoothly the video flows. I personally use DaVinci as it is free is really good in my opinion.  It is one of the best free editing software you can record through PS4 and are able to save it by first pressing the option button and then press save. Now you will be able to render the video later onto your laptop whenever you want. However, as easy as it is you can always use any software that you are comfortable with.
